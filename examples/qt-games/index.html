Our Story

Small Studio,
Big Ambition

Founded in 2020, QT Games started as two developers in a garage with a shared obsession: making games that feel genuinely new. Today we're a team of 15, still small enough to take creative risks but experienced enough to ship polish.

We believe the best games come from tight teams with strong vision. No committee design. No trend-chasing. Just honest craft and a relentless focus on player joy.
